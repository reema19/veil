# VEIL

VEIL is an iOS experience designed to help people reconnect with familiar places through quiet sensory awareness.

Instead of pushing users toward productivity, tracking, or constant interaction, VEIL creates space for noticing — the sounds, light, atmosphere, and subtle details that often disappear through repetition and routine.

The app encourages users to revisit the same places over time and capture simple sensory observations, transforming overlooked moments into meaningful experiences.

---

## Concept

Many everyday environments slowly become emotionally invisible to us.

Not because they changed, but because repetition trains our attention to stop noticing them.

VEIL explores how technology can gently support presence without interruption, pressure, or aggressive behavioral control.

The experience is intentionally calm, minimal, and user-initiated.

## Experience Flow

1. User selects a familiar place
2. User must be physically present at the location
3. User chooses a sensory direction (sight or sound)
4. VEIL generates a gentle observation prompt
5. User captures a sensory observation
6. Observations later become reflective “memories”

---

## Design Principles

VEIL is built around:

* Awareness without pressure
* Minimal interruption
* Emotional subtlety
* Familiarity over novelty
* Sensory engagement
* Calm interaction patterns

## Privacy Policy for VEIL

Effective Date: 2026

VEIL is designed to help users observe and reconnect with familiar places through sensory moments such as photos, audio recordings, and reflections.

## Data Collection

VEIL does not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

The app may ask for access to location, camera, microphone, and photo-related features only to provide its core experience. Location is used to help users choose and observe a familiar place. Camera access is used to capture observation photos. Microphone access is used to record observation sounds.

## User Content

Photos, audio recordings, place names, and observations created inside VEIL are stored locally on the user’s device. VEIL does not upload this content to an external server.

## Third-Party Sharing

VEIL does not share user data with advertisers, analytics companies, or third-party tracking services.

Children’s Privacy

VEIL is not designed to collect personal information from children.
